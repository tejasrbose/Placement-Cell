Our Mission


At AISSMS IOIT, our primary mission is to empower our students and provide them with the tools they need to embark on successful careers. We understand that your journey from education to employment is a crucial phase, and we are here to guide you every step of the way.


Our Commitment


Career Excellence: We are committed to nurturing talent and ensuring that you have the skills and confidence to excel in your chosen field. Industry Connections: Our strong network of industry partners and recruiters enables us to connect you with the right opportunities. Personalized Guidance: We understand that each student is unique. That's why we offer personalized career counseling and guidance tailored to your aspirations. Skill Development: Our placement programs focus on enhancing your employability by offering training in areas such as resume building, interview preparation, and soft skills. Success Stories: Our success stories are a testament to the impact we've made on countless students who have gone on to achieve remarkable success in their careers.


Why Choose AISSMS IOIT Placement Cell?


When you choose AISSMS IOIT for your placement needs, you are choosing a team that is passionate about your success. We are not just preparing you for a job; we are preparing you for a fulfilling and rewarding career. Our commitment to excellence, industry connections, and personalized support sets us apart. Join us on this journey, and together, we will pave the way to a bright and prosperous future.

The college has established a full fledged Training & Placement Department, to encompass various activities from shaping the students to meet the stringent requirements of the Industry, and to make them suitable to excel in the highly competitive business world.


The institute believes that every student who joins the institution deserves to get a potential earning opportunity. The main objective of T & P cell is to motivate and guide students to explore the domains of various career opportunities, entrepreneurship and enhance functional skills by providing industrial training,industry associated projects & enhance employability skills by honing aptitude skills, communication skills,GD & PI skills. The placement cell is dedicated to identify probable employers & conduct campus interviews. We also invite experts from industries to guide the students on latest technology, expectations from industries and different career options. T & P cell also acts as information source for opportunity in higher education and their qualifying examinations such as GATE, CAT, GRE etc.


Number of students placed in campus interviews is growing year by year. Many companies regularly visit institute for campus recruitment. Our students have been recruited by esteemed companies like: TCS, Oracle, Accenture, L & T, Wipro, SKF, Godrej, Tech Mahindra, , Emerson etc.
